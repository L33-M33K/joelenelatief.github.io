Origins


Joelene Jane Cakes is my custom cake business that I began in the 9th grade, and run currently. I work with Cakes4Kidz to bake and deliver birthday cakes to underserved youth in the Bay Area. It may be a small thing, but I hope to deliver a smile with every cake - delivering the cake and seeing the recipient's reaction is always the best part.

I also with with the Samaritan house on a yearly basis, creating cupcake displays to fundraise at their annual donor gala.
